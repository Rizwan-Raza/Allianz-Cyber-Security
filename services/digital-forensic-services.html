Digital Forensic Services


As specialists in Digital Forensics, allianz will perform analysis and examinations for government agencies, solicitors and commercial organizations in the event of a crime or unauthorized access to information systems.



Knowing where to look. Knowing how to look.


When an intrusion occurs it is our job to help you investigate. Our Digital Forensic Services team arms you with a set of investigators who know how to find and follow the digital trail that cyber-criminals leave behind.

Where things went wrong.


During the forensic investigation, allianz works hand-in-hand with your team to mitigate risk by allowing proper containment of incidents - we study the information we discover and work with you to understand where the security system failed and to improve handling of the event. We look at your existing security handling plans and make suggestions for improvement.

Meet security compliance requirements


It is very important that a complete forensic examination is conducted and a comprehensive report is kept on file to not only meet compliance requirements but to also prove a proper response was generated. A breach exposes your company data to hackers. If you fail to have a proper investigation and take corrective action - you are potentially open to litigation if sensitive information is taken.
