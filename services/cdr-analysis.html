CDR Analysis


Call monitoring and recording applications used by telecommunication companies generate extremely large amount of call detail records (CDRs) in real-time, and companies constantly need to leverage from this data to boost productivity. The volume of the calls and data captured by the call monitoring applications is so large that it impossible to manually analyse and conclude the behaviour of the network.

CDR Analysis takes call data records from a variety of sources and provides tools to identify patterns and trends, as well as look for suspicious patterns in calls which may indicate fraud.
